Founded with the mission to bridge the credit gap in the informal sector, Aye Finance leverages a hybrid “phygital” model—combining physical branch presence with advanced digital tools like AI and ML—to underwrite and manage small-ticket loans (average loan size: INR 1.5 lakh) to businesses lacking formal credit histories.As of 30 September 2025, the company operates 568 branches across 21 states and 3 union territories, servicing over 5 lakh active customers.
