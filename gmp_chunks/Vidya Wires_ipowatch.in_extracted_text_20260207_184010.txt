Business Overview
Aye Finance started its journey in 1993 and has been involved in the business of offering secured and unsecured small business loans for working capital, including mortgage loans, ‘Saral’ Property Loans, and secured and unsecured hypothecation loans, primarily to micro-scale MSMEs.
Furthermore, the company is ready to provide a business loan so the business can grow, and as collateral assets or property of the business.
It provides loans to businesses like manufacturing, trading, services, and agriculture-related businesses. The company helps 586,825 active customers in 18 states and 3 union territories and manages a lot of money for them. Aye finance provide loans, including mortgage loans, secured hypothecation loans, and unsecured hypothecation loans.
