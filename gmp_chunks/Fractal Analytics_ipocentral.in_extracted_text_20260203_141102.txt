As of 31 March 2025, the company operates under two key segments—Fractal.ai, which delivers AI services and products primarily hosted on its flagship platform Cogentiq, and Fractal Alpha, which manages independent AI businesses.Fractal serves 113 “Must Win Clients” (MWCs), including global enterprises such as Citi, Nestlé, Mars, and Philips, with its top ten clients contributing 53.8% of Fractal.ai’s revenue in Fiscal 2025. The firm’s expertise spans consumer packaged goods and retail (CPGR), technology, media and telecom (TMT), healthcare and life sciences (HLS), and banking, financial services, and insurance (BFSI).Recognized by Everest Group and Forrester as a leader in AI and analytics, Fractal has developed proprietary foundation models such as Kalaido.ai, Vaidya.ai, and Fathom-R1-14B, alongside pioneering initiatives like Project Ramanujan.
