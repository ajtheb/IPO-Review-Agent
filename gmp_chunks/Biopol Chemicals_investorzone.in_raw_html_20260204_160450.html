PAN HR Solution Limited was originally incorporated as on December 23, 2015. The Company operates on a business-to-business (B2B) model and provides comprehensive manpower and human resource solutions across India. Its services cater to roles ranging from unskilled to skilled blue-collar workers, enabling clients to streamline hiring processes, reduce administrative burden and ensure regulatory compliance.

The Company has a PAN-India presence and serves customers across sectors such as e-commerce, logistics, manufacturing, information technology and other industries.

Service Offerings

PAN HR Solution Limited offers an integrated suite of workforce and compliance solutions, including:

i) Manpower services
ii) Payroll management services
iii) Facility management services
iv) Staffing solutions
v) Compliance audit services

As part of its facility management services, the Company provides personnel such as housekeeping staff, pantry staff, office assistants and other support roles. Its comprehensive service portfolio enables the delivery of customized solutions tailored to client-specific requirements.

Operating Model and Workforce Deployment

The Company primarily operates under a Collect and Pay model, wherein invoices are raised on customers or principal employers for services rendered, followed by disbursement of payments to deployed personnel. In addition, the Company is in the process of gradually implementing a Pay and Collect model, under which advance payments are made to personnel and subsequently recovered from clients in accordance with contractual terms. As of November 30, 2025, the Company had 10,374 personnel deployed across various customer locations in India.

Client Base and Market Presence

Till date, the Company has served approximately 25 customers, comprising established and reputed companies with national and regional operations. The Company has received recognition and awards from its clients for service quality and performance.

Competitive Strengths

i) PAN-India workforce deployment capability
ii) Strong compliance and regulatory expertise
iii) Experienced promoter-led management team
iv) Comprehensive end-to-end service offerings
v) Ability to deliver customized workforce solutions across industries
